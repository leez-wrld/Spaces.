About Spaces

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Spaces began with a simple mission: to create beautiful, functional furniture that transforms houses into homes. Our founder, Emma Chen, started the company after struggling to find high-quality, aesthetically pleasing furniture for her own home.

What began as a small workshop has grown into a global brand, but our core values remain the same. We believe in craftsmanship, sustainability, and creating pieces that stand the test of time.

Our Approach

Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed and meticulously crafted. We work with skilled artisans who share our passion for quality and attention to detail. Our materials are responsibly sourced, and we're committed to reducing our environmental footprint.

We believe that beautiful spaces should be accessible to everyone, which is why we offer furniture at various price points without compromising on quality or design.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable practices, from responsibly sourced materials to eco-friendly packaging.

Craftsmanship

Our furniture is built to last, with attention to detail and quality materials that stand the test of time.

Community

We believe in creating spaces that bring people together and foster connection and belonging.
